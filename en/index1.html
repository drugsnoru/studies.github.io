Smoking is a serious abomination to human and social health. Addiction develops quickly. The major consequences include premature death and morbidity from cardiovascular disease, lung cancer and many other types of cancer, and COPD (chronic obstructive pulmonary disease). All citizens who use tobacco should be encouraged to quit and offered help and/or referrals to increase their success in quitting.

Shuddering from vaping (electronic cigarettes)   Vaping is when you use a portable electronic device to inhale mist ("steam") into your lungs. Evali (evali) - a disease of the lungs of a smoker  
Evali is a serious lung disease caused by vaping. Vitamin E acetate, found in some e-liquids, is a possible cause.
The Evali outbreak in late 2019 and early 2020 sent thousands of people to the hospital. At least 68 people died. EVALI cases have since declined, but people who vape can still get Evali.
Among those hospitalized with severe Evali, most were under 35 and had used THC-containing vapes from informal sources (online, family, or friends). However, EVALI can occur in anyone who uses either nicotine or THC-containing vapes. Read more…

Girls and Smoking   Smoking is a major public health and social problem worldwide. It is also a leading cause of preventable illness and death. More than 8 million people die each year from tobacco use worldwide. Half of people who smoke die from tobacco-related diseases, and half of these deaths occur in middle age. Even though smoking prevalence has historically been higher among men, many countries have seen an increase in smoking among young women compared to men.with young men. Read more…

The effects of smoking on humans   The effects of tobacco smoke on the respiratory system include:
✔ irritation of the trachea (windpipe) and larynx (voice box)
✔ decreased lung function and shortness of breath due to swelling and narrowing of the lung airways and excess mucus in the lung passages
✔ disruption of the lung cleansing system, leading to the accumulation of toxic substances, which causes irritation and damage to the lungs
