Every person, whose family suffered such grief as the presence of drunkenness, is perfectly known all the symptoms of alcoholism. But it will be useful to know about them and other people who want to save themselves and their loved ones from possible aggression of an alcoholic.

The first symptoms of alcoholism

In the early stages of alcoholism, a person may look normal and even happy. Only he feels some strange, but really very scary changes. The first symptoms of alcoholism are a lack of alcohol. An aspiring alcoholic feels uncomfortable if he does not have at least a few shots of alcohol in his hands or stomach. Of course, he goes to work, cleans houses, and even plays with children. But inside, emptiness is formed, despondency and depression attack. Just the thought that they will soon be able to pour in another portion of poison gives them some revival.
Such a simple example perfectly demonstrates - alcohol and all alcohol-containing drinks are a kind of drug. Yes, they are planted not in a few days, but in weeks, months, and sometimes years. But in such a case, it is enough just to start. It is almost never possible to finish without harm to yourself and others.

Alcoholism with advanced symptoms


If alcoholism has not been stopped (or simply prevented by complete refusal to take alcohol) in the first stages, it noticeably "pumps". And so, a young, sporty guy who yesterday tried beer for company with classmates to be "as an adult", today there are symptoms of chronic alcoholism.
At the same time, a person does not just feel discomfort in a sober state - he enters into rage. For him, sobriety is like torture - all his thoughts come down to one thing, where he could get vodka (brandy, wine, whiskey, moonshine, technical alcohol, triple cologne) and fall back into semi-forgetfulness. At first, he stops controlling how much he drinks. Starting with one or two "appetite" glasses, after a few months he can drink a couple of bottles of vodka for a sitting "for health".

Later, other symptoms of alcohol dependence develop. An alcoholic stops controlling the quality of the alcohol he drinks. He could start with a collectible brandy, a few hundred dollars or even a thousand dollars a bottle, and now he's ready to drink any alcohol-containing substance. Hence the huge mortality among alcoholics - moonshine of poor quality cleaning, technical alcohols and other poisons rapidly destroy almost the entire human body.

Unmotivated aggression can also be included in the symptoms of alcoholism. Thus, wives and children of alcoholics often walk with beatings. Killings are also common when trying to argue with an alcoholic. A creature that has recently been human is not going to listen to advice, try to change itself. He is satisfied with a crazy, semi-animal state, when the past and the future disappear, and the present is reduced to a glass filled with alcohol poison. The lion's share of accidents, domestic quarrels and murders, fights and other crimes is committed in a state of alcoholic intoxication. A drunkard may rush at a passing person because the person looks wrong, is not dressed or simply does not look so. Education, politeness, simple common sense - all this an alcoholic leaves in his past life. In the same one that the early symptoms of alcoholism were shared with the present.

Symptoms of alcoholism for beer lovers


Alas, many people around the world believe that alcoholics are people who abuse spirits - vodka, cognac, whiskey. But there are two mistakes at once. First, any drinking is already EVIL. Second, beer alcoholism is no less common and far more dangerous.
The fact is that a man with a glass of vodka in his hand can still be perceived as an alcoholic, but a man with a bottle of beer, thanks to the efforts of the media, cinema and literature, is perceived as a successful, strong, intelligent and deft hero. Of course, this is not the case - such stereotypes are formed by beer producers who make tens of billions of dollars in profit, slowly but surely destroying entire populations.

Symptoms of alcohol use disorder are less noticeable than those of vodka and cognac. A person looks almost normal, except that he inadvertently drinks beer - on the street, at home, in a bar, in stadiums, in a cinema and in general anywhere. Occasionally, such individuals drink up to 10-12 liters of beer a day. But he doesn't always lie in puddles of dirt, falls under the feet of passers-by. Therefore, the danger of beer alcoholism is especially high - many people do not recognize it as a real threat. And she's scary.
Just look at the "men" who love to indulge themselves with a liter-another beer after work. Can they be called men? Weak, helpless creatures with huge bellies - they cannot stand up for themselves and their family in case of danger. The large amount of the female hormone estrogen that is contained in beer makes it loose, indecisive, lazy. The pectoral muscles swell and droop, the abdomen enlarges, and the hips expand.
Symptoms of female alcoholism are also not very pleasant for beer lovers. Excess hormone has a negative effect - women become more aggressive, the menstrual cycle is disrupted, scandals and tantrums often occur.

How to avoid all this?

After reading about the symptoms of alcohol addiction, many people think - how to avoid it? Is it even possible? There can be only one answer - of course! And it's very easy to do that. It's enough just to give up alcohol. COMPLETELY! ONCE AND FOR ALL! COMPLETELY!
This is much easier than it might seem. There will be a lot of free time, which can be filled with travel, adventure, sports, creativity, self-development, which really allows you to become better, stronger and smarter.
A decent amount of money is saved. And for them you can find a much more suitable and pleasant investment.
Someone hesitates to stop drinking and say it out loud, for fear of losing friends. Yes, many friends will be lost - what can a sleepy, weak-willed drunk and a strong, determined and intelligent person have in common? But your remaining friends are those with whom you share a common vision and a true friendship, not a bottle of vodka. Therefore, it is they who will not fail in the difficult situation when drinking bottles will turn away.

Of course, with this approach, you will never know the symptoms of chronic alcoholism. In the morning your head will not be sore, your hands will not be shaking, your wife and children will not be afraid of you, and respect in the society of strong and intelligent people will always be yours.
So, only you can choose to follow your path, dictated to you by common sense, or the easy path, which laid for you drug sellers, having learned the alcoholism, the symptoms of which are listed above, numerous diseases and early death from cirrhosis.
