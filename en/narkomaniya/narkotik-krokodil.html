The drug "crocodile" is also desomorphine - an artisanal drug refers to synthetic opiates, in comparison with morphine, it is more likely to cause long-term dependence. Drug addiction to the drug "crocodile" develops after 2 intravenous injections. Psychic dependence on the use of the drug "crocodile" develops faster than physical, after the first injection of the drug "crocodile" there is a strong desire to repeat the experience.
The drug "crocodile" is also called the drug of the poor, since its preparation is quite cheap, as soon as the addict runs out of money for heroin, he switches to the drug "crocodile".
"Crocodile" drug addiction is a fairly young type of drug addiction, crocodile drug addiction became widespread after 2000, and rapid spread occurred after 2005. Crocodile drug addiction stands in 2nd place after heroin addiction.
Why has crocodile become so popular with addicts? In the 2000s, an intense drug war began, which led to the disappearance of heroin, cocaine and other drugs from major cities, forcing addicts to look for other ways to enjoy themselves. There was a paradox related to the good work of the drug police.

Recipe for the drug "crocodile"

The composition of the drug "crocodile" includes codeine containing preparations, gasoline, iodine, household solvents, sulfuric acid, sulfur containing in matchstick heads and phosphorus. The preparation of the drug "crocodile" uses highly toxic substances with an impurity of heavy metals. It turns out a killer mixture, which leads to death in just a few months.
Why was the drug called "crocodile"? The name comes from the fact that the injection site has sores and erosions, which are initially covered with a purulent crust, the skin in the affected areas becomes similar to the skin of a crocodile.
